Our practice

Ways of Working

Clarify & Launch – Foundation Phase

For when things feel unclear, heavy, or stuck – Before you invest time and money in the wrong direction, this phase brings clarity. We assess what you have, define what you need, and deliver a clear platform, identity, or strategy you can move forward with. Ideal for new ventures, brand refreshes, or teams that have outgrown their current setup.

Grow – Strategic Evolution

For when you're ready to scale without creating chaos - We help you improve and expand your digital platform, brand, and experience in a structured way. By setting up systems, guidelines, and templates, we reduce the need for constant creative oversight. This includes design, development, maintenance, and ongoing guidance.

Evolve – Ongoing Partnership

For teams who want long-term clarity and support - When continuity matters, we stay closely involved as a trusted partner — guiding, adjusting, and protecting the integrity of your brand and platform over time. Think of it as a creative and strategic layer that stays with you.

Advise – Strategic Advisory

For leadership teams facing complex digital choices - We act as an independent sounding board for major decisions — whether it's choosing a platform, evaluating a rebrand, or restructuring your digital operation. You get senior perspective, honest feedback, and structured guidance, without having to hire full-time leadership. Ideal for founders, directors, and executive teams.

Erik Kessels and Gallerie d'Italia - Artist and Exhibition Space

Creative Production & Technical Direction

Collaborated with artist and creative director Erik Kessels on Un'Immagine, a large-scale immersive installation presented at Gallerie d'Italia, Milan, opening October 2024.

The work draws on thousands of images from the Publifoto Archive, algorithmically stitched and morphed into a continuously moving 360-degree visual and sound experience, with an original soundtrack by Robin Rimbaud and Stefano Pilia.

The core challenge was translating a precise artistic vision into a technically complex output without losing its intent in the process. Working directly with Erik, I identified the right technical collaborator, then acted as the bridge between artistic direction and execution — interpreting the concept, managing the creative dialogue, and overseeing delivery to Gallerie d'Italia.

The exhibition opened to critical success.

Our principles

Form follows function

We begin with purpose before form. Every decision is shaped by use, context, and accessibility. Structure comes first, aesthetics follow. Simplicity is not reduction for its own sake, it is the result of systems designed to perform clearly and reliably in real conditions.

True materials

We work with what is real and sustainable for the client. Strategy, technology, and content must reflect your organisation's reality and ambitions. We select tools and structures for their durability, transparency, and long-term relevance, not for short-term appeal.

Smart use of resources

We treat time, budget, and attention as strategic assets. Our work is planned to minimise waste, avoid unnecessary complexity, and prioritise decisions that deliver lasting value. Efficiency, in our view, is a form of respect.

Accessibility & Universality

Quality is measured by who can use it. We design systems that are inclusive, legible, and adaptable across abilities, devices, and contexts. Accessibility is embedded from the start, as a foundation for resilience, compliance, and long-term trust.
