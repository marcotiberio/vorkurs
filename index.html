What we do

Design Systems

We build component libraries that work in Figma and in code. Auto Layout, tokens, variables -- structured so your team can use them without breaking things.

Not a one-off delivery. A system that scales with you.

Creative Direction & UI Design

We set the visual direction -- brand translation, art direction, interface design -- and then we build it. Next.js, Nuxt, React, Shopify, Webflow, Framer.

What you approved in Figma is exactly what goes live.

Strategy & Audits

Sometimes you don't need a new site. You need someone to look at what you have, find the gaps between design and code, and give you a plan.

We do that too -- usually in a week.

Stewardship

After launch, things drift. New pages get added, components get misused, the system starts to erode.

We stay on as a retained partner to keep things tight. Monthly check-ins, component reviews, quality control.

How we work together

Audit – Fixed scope

For when something feels off but you're not sure what. We spend a week inside your Figma files and codebase, map the gaps between them, and give you a clear plan. Fixed fee, no commitment beyond that.

Embedded – Monthly retainer

For teams that need the bridge full-time. We join your team 2-3 days a week and own the design system -- in Figma and in code. This is how most long-term partnerships start.

Project – End to end

For when you need a site designed and built. We take it from brief to launch. Brand translation, UI design, design system, frontend, deployment. One team, one process, no handoff gaps.

Advisory – Fractional lead

For teams with designers and developers who aren't aligned. We come in as a fractional design engineering lead. Set up the system, define the workflow, train the team, step back. You get senior perspective without a full-time hire.

Principles

Function first

Every decision -- layout, type, spacing, interaction -- should solve a problem. If it doesn't serve the user, it doesn't ship. Structure comes first, aesthetics follow.

One person, no handoff

Most projects lose fidelity in the gap between design and development. We eliminate that gap by doing both. What we design is what we build.

Systems over pages

We don't make websites. We make systems that produce websites. Components, tokens, rules -- structured so the next 50 pages are as consistent as the first 5.

Built to last

Clean code, accessible markup, fast load times. No shortcuts. We build things that work in two years, not just on launch day.
